Hello, World

This is a placeholder post, which purpose is to help me configure this blog – but I may as well use it to briefly introduce myself. I’ve played TTRPGs when I was a teenager, and then went on a hiatus of around 15 years. When I decided to give it another go, I couldn’t find any group who could take me as a player so I just said what the hell and jumped into GMing. I’ve spent about 5 years GMing two D&D 5e campaigns, until I’ve had enough and decided to move on from it. I decided to create this blog because I finally feel like I have something to share with the wider community. I feel like I have a somewhat interesting perspective: I am no longer a noob but neither am I a highly experience GM or a trained designer. I feel like the voices of people professionals and hardcore fans is a bit drowning nowadays in the online TTRPGs community, and the view of someone who is less invested in this hobby – but still invested enough to dedicate himself to write a blog about it – would be a valuable addition to the conversation.
